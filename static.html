Project 2: Netflix Movies

Investigating possible contributing factors for short movies from previous years to nowadays. Based on analysis of Netflix movies, I have determined that the release year does not appear to be a significant factor influencing the duration of movies. My findings suggest that the year in which a movie is released does not predict whether it will be a short film or not. This implies that other factors may play a more crucial role in determining the length of movies on Netflix.
